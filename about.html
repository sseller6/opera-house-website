About Our Opera House

Built in 1920, this historic opera house has featured many amazing plays, and it's open once again!

Mission

Our Mission is to get kids up and out and doing things, getting a little culture in the arts, working with the community to provide something taht will allow those in the area to develop their talents, skills, and be able to use the venue in ways that it was originally intended to bring family and friends and the community together.

History

The building was restored in 1985 and remains a cultural landmark in the city.
